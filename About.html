“Login Junction” is a website which helps its user to access authentic login pages of various social media, banks, mail etc.

It is not easy to remember the site or IP addresses of all the website we use. br
This project will enable user to safely login to their various accounts from a single place and remove the hassle of remembering site addresses along with saving them from being a victim of Phishing attacks.

Till this date, Phishing remains a popular way of stealing people’s login credentials, debit/credit card details, committing fraud and distributing viruses.

Phishing is a cyber-attack that uses disguised email or links as a weapon.
The goal is to trick the recipient into believing that the message is something they want or is needed urgently which maybe in form of a bill which is due, a request from their credit card provider or bank or any link from unknown source which redirects them to some unauthenticated site.

In 2020, there have been more than 241,324 incidents of phishing attacks.

The main objective behind this project is to protect and educate people to stay safe online. Many people are not aware about which sites are authentic and which are not secure.

My website – “Login Junction” provides one stop shop for all the login pages, be it for a bank, a social media account, an email or even a travel/booking site.
This website will help users to login safely to their respective accounts without the fear of being a victim of a phishing attack.

The site also contains a Learn section where users can gain insights and knowledge on how to stay safe online and protect themselves from such scams and phishing attacks.

This website will also remove the hassle of remembering all the website and domain name
